By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## Our Approach: Offline-First

HaraZone is designed as an **offline-first** application. The vast majority of your data — saved places, preferences, notes, and discovery history — lives on your device and stays on your device. The App is fully functional without an internet connection once an area has been loaded.

Our backend services are minimal and exist only to support features that inherently require connectivity, such as AI-powered recommendations and push notifications. We do not maintain user profiles, social graphs, or behavioral databases on our servers.

---

## Information We Collect

### Device Location

The App uses your device's GPS to find nearby places and display them on the map. Your location is processed on-device and is **not stored on our servers**. Location data is sent to third-party map and AI services only as needed to fulfill your requests (e.g., showing nearby places, generating recommendations).

### Chat Messages

When you use the AI chat feature, your messages are sent to the Google Gemini API to generate responses. We do not store, log, or retain your chat messages on any server we operate. Google's handling of this data is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Saved Places and Notes

Places you save and notes you write are stored **locally on your device** using an on-device database (SQLite). This data is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly choose to sync or share it in the future.

### Push Notifications

If you opt in to push notifications, your device's push token is stored on our backend to deliver alerts (e.g., events at saved places, best-time-to-visit reminders). We do not associate push tokens with personal identifiers. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

### Crash Reports

The App uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports. These reports contain technical information about the crash (device model, OS version, stack traces) and do not include personally identifiable information. See [Firebase Privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).

### Analytics

The App uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage patterns (e.g., feature usage, session duration). This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users. See [Firebase Privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).

### Bug Reports

Bug reports are user-initiated only. When you choose to submit a bug report, it is sent via your device's email client and may include a screenshot and recent app logs. You control what is sent and can review the email before sending.

## Our Backend Services

Our backend is intentionally minimal. It handles only:

- **Push notification delivery** — routing alerts to opted-in devices
- **Real-time event data** — surfacing live events and time-sensitive information for the ambient ticker

The backend does not store user profiles, conversation history, location history, or any data that could identify you. All persistent user data remains on your device.
