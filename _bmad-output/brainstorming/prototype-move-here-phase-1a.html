Traveler chat
← Lisbon Travel guide
What's the food scene like?
Lisbon's food scene is incredible! 🎉 You HAVE to try pastéis de nata at Manteigaria — they're warm out of the oven. For seafood, hit up Cervejaria Ramiro — the garlic prawns are legendary. Time Out Market is great for variety. Don't miss bifana sandwiches from a street vendor!

Resident chat (persona shift)
← Living in Lisbon 🏠 Relocation advisor
What's the food scene like?
Lisbon has great food, but here's the resident reality: eating out regularly adds up. A typical lunch menu is €8-12, dinner €15-25.
📊 Groceries are ~30% cheaper than Miami. Monthly grocery budget for a couple: €300-400.
For daily cooking, Pingo Doce and Continente are the go-to supermarkets — both have locations near Cais do Sodré. The Mercado da Ribeira (behind Time Out Market) sells fresh produce directly from farmers at much better prices than tourist restaurants.
