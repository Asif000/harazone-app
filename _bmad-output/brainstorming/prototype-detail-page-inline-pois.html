Inline POI Card Design Decisions

  • Vertical inline — cards stack in the chat flow like messages. No horizontal carousel. Natural scroll.
  • Full width (92% of chat area) — cards are prominent but clearly distinct from text bubbles via card styling (border, shadow, thumbnail).
  • Status stripe on left edge — immediate open/closed signal without reading text. Green/orange/red/grey.
  • Satellite thumbnail — reuses map tile data. Gives spatial context. Dark image on light card creates visual anchor.
  • AI insight line — not generic description. Gemini generates a 1-2 line hook specific to context ("24-hour spot where cops end up" vs "Cuban restaurant").
  • Action row — Save, Navigate, Ask AI. "Ask AI" opens that POI's detail page with auto-seeded context.
  • Tapping card body navigates to that POI's detail page (pushes onto nav stack).
  • Cards interleaved with text — AI can put text before cards (context), between cards (comparison), and after cards (advice). Not a separate "card section."
