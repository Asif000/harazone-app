Card Stack — Stacked beige cards on white

Each bucket is a distinct card with icon header. Cards stack vertically with clear separation. Scroll to explore deeper. Most similar to a news feed layout.

+ Clear bucket separation · Easy to scan · Familiar pattern · Natural sharing unit (each card)
− Cards create visual rhythm breaks · More vertical space used by card chrome

Continuous Scroll — Apple Weather model

One continuous reading experience with subtle section dividers. Orange header with time context. Highlight facts in beige callout boxes break up the text flow. Bottom nav for switching views.

+ Most readable for long-form · Natural flow · Highlight facts draw eye · Time context in header
− Less visual separation between buckets · Header takes screen space

Tabbed Buckets — One bucket at a time

Horizontal tabs let users jump to any bucket. Each tab shows deep content for one bucket. Reduces overwhelm by showing one topic at a time. White fact cards on beige background.

+ Zero overwhelm · Deep focus per bucket · Quick bucket switching · Great for reference
− Breaks the "story unfolding" feeling · User must actively navigate · Streaming reveal less natural

Story Mode — Instagram Stories meets area portraits

Each bucket is a full-screen "story" with progress dots. Swipe horizontally between buckets. One bucket fills the entire screen for immersive reading. Actions at the bottom of each story.

+ Most immersive · Zero overwhelm · Familiar swipe pattern · Great for sharing individual buckets
− Can't scan all buckets at once · Requires 6 swipes for full portrait · Streaming reveal per-story only
