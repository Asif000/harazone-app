The beating heart of Marrakech's medina. By day, a maze of orange juice stalls and snake charmers. By night, it transforms into an open-air theatre of food vendors, storytellers, and musicians under lantern light.
🕐 WHY NOW
It's late afternoon — the square is shifting from daytime bazaar to evening food market. Golden hour light right now.
💡 LOCAL TIP ↻
Stall 14 and Stall 1 have the best lamb tagine. Walk past the first row of vendors — better food deeper in.
⚠ Safety Advisory
Exercise Increased Caution
Petty theft and pickpocketing common in crowded areas. Keep valuables secured. Be aware of tourist scams. Avoid unlicensed guides.
